Join Midwest AF via Patreon

Membership to Midwest AF ranges from $2/month to $10/month.


If this doesn't work for your budget, send Rebecca Stavick an email and ask for a free membership and you'll get an invite, no questions asked.

Why Should I Join?

Join Midwest AF to support our mission to drive change and progress in the Midwest.

Read more about our mission.

  • Get connected to a network of people shaking things up across the Midwest who are facing similar challenges and finding solutions that may benefit us all.
  • Membership grants you access to our private discourse forum, which is a safe space to discuss the issues that matter to us. This forum is moderated and protected by a Code of Conduct.
  • If you want to have a positive impact on your community, but you're not sure where to invest your energy, Midwest AF has projects and ideas that you can plug into right away, along with a supportive network to help you along the way.
  • Members get to publish on our Midwest AF Medium publication.
  • All members are empowered to host meetups across the Midwest. Host your own meetup and events locally with Midwest AF resources.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn't membership free?

  • A small monthly fee incentivizes members to participate or cancel. Members need to have skin in the game for this community to be successful.
  • There are costs associated with managing the club, including discourse forum hosting fees, and the ongoing costs of outreach, promotion, etc.
  • We employ a part-time community organizer to amplify our work.
  • Every year, we will have a secret party just for our members. Stay tuned!

I'd like to join as a recruiter or to officially promote my organization. Can I join?

We do not offer organizational memberships at this time. If you want to chat, contact Rebecca Stavick.
