Midwest Tech Workers Anonymous Wage Sharing Survey

If you are a technologist or technical worker, please consider sharing your compensation via this 100% anonymous survey. This initiative, led by Naomi See, is designed to shed light on the state of pay and benefits in the tech industry in the Midwest. Discussing this issue helps to fight pay discrimination, which is a force that pushes women and people of color out of the tech industry.
