What Do We Do?

We Support Each Other

Our forum is a safe space full of people who support each other like a family. Every Friday, we give a huge collective shoutout on social media to support one of our members and expand their work, interests, or needs to our networks.

We Talk About Important Issues

We lead the discussion of what's going on in the Midwest and we discuss current events in the forum, on social media, and in person at meetups and events. Also run a Medium publication that members post to.

We Take Collective Action

We mobilize around issues that are ripe for disruption by speaking with one voice at the same time. We support each other in this way locally, and at a regional level. See below, initiatives.
